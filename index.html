Project Description

This project will focus on creating an end-to-end system that will detect the presence of fire from camera footage

in a timely and accurate manner, aiming to stop the fire before it has a chance to cause real damage.

Once the fire is identified, the system will then find the approximate placement of it. An alarm system will be activated

and the system will locate where the nearest fire extinguisher from pre-determined fire extinguisher stations and show its location.
